Introducction to Linkonnekt

Have you also felt utterly exhausted going on from someone’s house to another on Diwali or Christmas, and felt that you were wasting your time in a silly way, when you could have bonded with your near and dear ones. Have you ever realised, you haven’t thanked your secondary school teacher or your mentor or your landlord, enough? To the aforementioned problems, we have come up with a simple, yet beautifully crafted solution. We have thought about a portal wherein you can manage your relationships in a really easy and effective way. The portal allows you to send gifts and wishes on regular basis and, to an extent, automate the task.

More

On our portal, you can add people that you know (whom we prefer to call links) and allocate them budget according to 2 really simple factors:

  • The relation you want to maintain
  • And how much you know the person

and then we will help you automate some basic tasks that have to do with the relation you have with the person. These tasks include sending regular gifts to the person on special occasions like Diwali and New Year as well as sending Wishes on Birthdays and Anniversaries. These gifts and greetings would be sent via our Operations team in a remarkable fashion. The whole point of sending the greetings will be making sure the person remembers you in Hindsight.
